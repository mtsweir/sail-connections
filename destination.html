Quaint villages, historic towns and more…

Croatia is a popular yacht charter destination. Quaint villages, historic towns, peaceful island refuges and fascinating countryside to explore, with places of interest spaced at convenient sailing intervals. Your yacht charter will be a pleasant journey taking you to moorings on town quays, anchorages in quiet bays, and/or berths in modern marinas. In recent times this fantastic cruising ground has boomed. With so many operators and itineraries to choose from, the options are almost endless. Let us work through it all with you for the very best Croatian sailing holiday.
