Lagoon 52, Bareboat Catamaran Charter

In creating the 52, the people at Lagoon have excelled, making innovative and sometimes radical choices to deliver elegance, comfort, user-friendliness and, it goes without saying, top cruising performance under sail.

The Lagoon 52 marks a new era in the design of blue water cruising catamarans. The decision to position the mast further aft adds a wide possibility of sail combinations to an already impressive performance. For over a decade now the Lagoon designers have gradually been moving their masts aft, thus centering weight more evenly and improving performance of the sail plan. The Lagoon 52 was one of the first cruising catamarans to benefit from this development.

The main objective in creating the Lagoon 52 has been to improve the use of space. Bright and comfortable living areas suggest a ‘just like home’ feel for guests on board. Use of continuous horizontal lines without interruption gives the interior fluent and harmonious open spaces, while outside the sleek lines bring elegance from stem to stern, with a focus on comfort and safety.

The 52F (Flybridge) and 52S (SporTop) distinguish the two above-deck layouts.

The Lagoon 52 is available bareboat, crewed bareboat, and in several locations permanently crewed for the ultimate luxury sailing holiday.
